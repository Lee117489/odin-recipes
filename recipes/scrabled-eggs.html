Scrambled Eggs

Description

Quick, simple and delicious scrabled eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp of butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Put a frying pan on low heat
  2. Crack eggs to a pan
  3. Add butter
  4. Whisk. Every 30s take the pan of the heat. Keep whisking.
  5. When done add cheese
  6. Serve
