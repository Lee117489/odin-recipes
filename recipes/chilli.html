Chilli

Description

Loaded with both beef and pork, this chili is extra meaty. I keep it mild so it's easy on sensitive stomachs and for children who can't handle hot spices.

Ingredients

  • 0.5kg minced beef
  • 0.5kg minced pork
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 can of beer
  • 2 tbsp of ground cumin
  • 2 tbscp of chilli powder
  • 2 tsp of dried oregano
  • 1 tsp of cayenne powder
  • 2 can of tomato paste
  • 1 can of diced tomatos
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cans of beans
  • 500ml of beef broth

Steps

  1. Brown ground pork and ground beef.
  2. Drain.
  3. Add garlic to ground meat and saut√© 1 minute.
  4. Add water and beer to meat and garlic; bring to boil.
  5. Add cumin, chili powder, oregano, and cayenne pepper.
  6. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 10 minutes.
  7. Add tomato paste, sugar, diced tomatoes, peppers, beans and beef broth and simmer 30 minutes.
  8. If chili is too thick for you add some additional water.
